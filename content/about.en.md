## Overview

Professional with over 20 years of experience in designing and evolving complex IT infrastructures, focusing on hybrid architectures, modern workplace and resilient enterprise environments.

Operating at the intersection of technology and strategy, supporting public and private organizations in digital transformation and ensuring a constant balance between innovation, operational sustainability, and risk management.

---

## Areas of Responsibility

The role sits within the **Enterprise Architecture** domain, with cross-functional responsibilities covering:

- Definition of hybrid IT architectures *(on-premise + cloud)*
- Evolution of Modern Workplace and Digital Workspace
- Design of Private Cloud and virtualized platforms
- Integration of security, identity and compliance into architectural designs
- Definition of Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery strategies

---

## Architectural Approach

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Requirements-driven design** | Every choice is a function of real constraints: budget, compliance, operability |
| **Explicit trade-offs** | Every architecture is the result of conscious compromises |
| **Technology neutrality** | Solution selection based on context, not vendor |
| **Resilience by design** | Operational continuity and security integrated from the design phase |
| **Operational simplicity** | Reduction of unnecessary complexity |
